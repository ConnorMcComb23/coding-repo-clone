Architect Studio

inspired design for people

Architect Studio was fromed by James McNulty and Deirdre Morris in 2015 and are based in Moira, Co Down. We are Chartered Architects and we provide a full range of professional architectural services, from inital concept design through to the final project completion. Our mission is to be creative and each project is a unique attempt to create a realtionship between people and place through architecture. Our experience includes work across a range of sectors including:

  • Residential
  • Retail
  • Industrial
  • Sports
  • Community and Youth

Our main speciality is in residential architecture. Our desire to make a positive contribution to contemporary society drives our work. We are interested in the conservation of architectural heritage and sustainability with our work in This area featured in a number of press publications. We continually seek to expand our knowledge base and the depth of our expertise.

As our pratice has evolved, we have developed expertise across a number of themes. These include: developing site specific housing that respond to their location in the local countryside; re-imagining existing dwellings through refurbishment and extension; working sensitively on the conservation of lsited buildings; helping community organisations to develop the potential of their premises and facilities.

We are eager to meet any design challenge whatever the context might be. If you think your project will benefit from the application of our creative energy and our expertise in architecture, then please get in touch.
